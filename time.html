TRIALS SAFETY RULES

We have adopted the rules the Sports Car Club of America

Motorsports are dangerous. These Rules are in way a guarantee against injury or death to participants, spectators or others. You can reduce risk by driving well, properly using superior safety gear, attention, and reporting unsatisfactory issues to the event officials. Drivers are responsible ensuring the vehicle being used is properly prepared for operation under elevated acceleration, braking and cornering forces. Drivers must have a Inspection (Tech) Sheet completed and available to present to an Official.

Entrants are reminded that is a considerable gap between a standard and the best protection that current technology can providE. It is recommended seats, systems, roll bars and helmets meet the highest safety possiblE.

National Time and some SCCA Regions require more the minimum safety required by these Rules. In these cases, there are two additional Safety Levels.
